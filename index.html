The Strategy

How the BRRRR Strategy Works With Section 8 Housing

Each letter in BRRRR8 Academy represents a stage in the cycle. Master all five and you can scale a portfolio indefinitely without leaving equity locked in a single property.

B
Buy
Find the Deal
Target distressed properties priced below market value with strong Section 8 rent potential. Our deal analyzer tells you in minutes whether a property pencils out.
R
Rehab
Renovate Right
Scope, bid, and manage the rehab to HUD inspection standards. We show you exactly which finishes pass inspection and which improvements add the most appraised value.
R
Rent
Section 8 Tenant
Work with your local PHA to get listed, pass the HCV inspection, and place a vetted Section 8 tenant. The government pays a guaranteed portion every single month.
R
Refinance
Pull Your Capital
Use the stabilized rental income and updated appraisal to do a cash-out refinance. Get your original investment back — or close to it — and keep the property.
R8
Repeat
Build the Portfolio
Reinvest recovered capital into your next deal. Each cycle adds a cash-flowing Section 8 door to your portfolio with little-to-no capital permanently tied up.
